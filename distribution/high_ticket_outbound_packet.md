# HIGH-TICKET OUTBOUND PACKET

## Purpose
Direct outreach to qualified leads for premium services

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE

### Primary: Solopreneurs with money problems
- Running service business
- Making £5K-£20K/month
- Working 40+ hours
- No system/team

### Secondary: Consultants
- Freelance consultants
- Coaches
- Agencies
- Anyone billing £100+/hour

### Tertiary: Content creators
- YouTubers with 10K+ views
- Podcasters
- Bloggers with traction

---

## OUTBOUND MESSAGE SCRIPTS

### Script 1: Problem-Led (Cold)
"Hey [name], quick question - how many hours did you work on your business THIS WEEK?

If it's more than 40, you're not alone. Most solopreneurs are.

I help them systemize so they work 3 hours/day instead of 12. Saved one client £2,400/month.

If that's interesting, I can share what works. Just reply 'SYSTEM' and I'll send you the playbook."

---

### Script 2: Social Proof-Led (Warm)
"Hey [name] - saw your profile. The work you're doing with [project] looks solid.

Quick context: I help solopreneurs automate their business so they work LESS but make MORE. Saved my last client £2,400/month.

If that's relevant, I can share a few quick wins. Reply 'WIN' and I'll send you 5 automations you can implement today."

---

### Script 3: Direct Offer (Hot)
"Hey [name], I help solopreneurs who are drowning in their business.

The result: They go from working 15-hour days to 3 hours, without hiring anyone.

My process:
1. Audit where you're losing time
2. Build 5 custom AI workflows for YOUR business
3. Set everything up

It's a 3-day process, £1,500. Most clients see ROI in week 1.

Interested in a quick call to see if it's a fit?"

---

### Script 4: LinkedIn Connection Request
"I help solopreneurs save 12+ hours/week without hiring. Would love to share what's working."

[After connection follows with Script 1-3]

## RESPONSE HANDLING

### If they reply "interested":
- Schedule call within 24 hours
- Send calendar link or suggest times
- Pre-qualify: "Quick question - what's your biggest time drain right now?"

### If they reply "tell me more":
- Send value first: 5 quick wins
- Then pitch: "Want to hop on a call?"

### If no response:
- Follow up in 48 hours: "Hey - just following up. Still curious?"
