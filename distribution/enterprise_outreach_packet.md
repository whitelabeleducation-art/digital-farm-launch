# ENTERPRISE OUTREACH PACKET

## Target: Companies with £500K+ Revenue

Enterprise deals = fastest path to £100K (fewer closes needed).

---

## ENTERPRISE PROFILE

**Target companies:**
- £500K-£5M revenue
- 10-50 employees
- Ops-heavy (manufacturing, services, agencies)
- Have budget for improvement
- Technology adopters

**Contact decision-makers:**
- CEO / Founder
- COO / Operations Director
- CTO / Head of Ops

---

## OUTREACH SEQUENCE

### Email Template 1: The Cold Outreach

**Subject:** Quick question about [company's obvious pain]

```
[Name],

I'll be direct — I help companies like yours [specific outcome].

Question: how are you currently handling [their pain]? Manual processes? Contractors?

If you're spending more than [X] on [pain area], there's a better way.

I have one enterprise implementation slot available this [month/week].

Worth 15 minutes of your time?

No pitch if not a fit — just a conversation about your situation.

—
[Founder]
```

## ENTERPRISE CLOSE SCRIPTS

### Discovery Call Opener
"Tell me about [company] — what's the current state of [their pain area]?"

### Problem Quantification
"What's this costing you per month? Per year?"

### Solution Presentation
"Here's what we've done for similar companies — [case study]"

### Investment Discussion
"Based on what you've told me, this would be a £[X] investment. Here's what's included..."

### Close Attempt
"To move forward, we'd need [deposit] to reserve the implementation slot. When would you like to start?"

---

## ENTERPRISE OBJECTION HANDLING

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We have an agency" | "What are they not solving? What would you add if you could?" |
| "Not in budget" | "What's the cost of not solving this? What's the ROI?" |
| "Need to run by board" | "What would help them say yes? I can help make the case" |
| "Send proposal" | "Proposals are one-way. A call is two-way. Let's do a call." |
| "Call back later" | "What's the specific trigger for later? Can we pre-schedule?" |

## SAME-DAY ENTERPRISE PLAY

**If you get an enterprise meeting today:**

1. **Pre-call:** Research their company, prepare case study

4. **Follow-up:** If not close, schedule next step within 24 hours
