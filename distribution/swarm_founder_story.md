# FOUNDER STORY LAUNCH SWARM

## Objective
Humanize the brand, build trust through authentic story.

---

## Swarming Content Types

### Story 1: The Pain (Day 1)
**Post:** "I burned out in 2024. 80-hour weeks. $3,000/month on contractors. No time for my actual business. That's when I built AI agents to do the work for me."

**Angle:** Raw honesty
**Objective:** Connect with pain
**CTA:** "Here's the system: [LINK]"

---

### Story 2: The Realization (Day 2)
**Post:** "The moment I realized I was working for my business instead of ON it: I calculated how much I was paying contractors vs how much I was making. The math was brutal."

**Angle:** Wake-up call
**Objective:** Validate their pain
**CTA:** Try the free bundle

---

### Story 3: The Solution (Day 3)
**Post:** "So I built 5 AI agents to handle: Research, Content, Outreach, Data, Support. Now I work 3 hours/day. I saved $2,400/month. And I'm giving the system away."

**Angle:** Solution reveal
**Objective:** Present offer
**CTA:** Get the bundle

---

### Story 4: The Results (Day 4)
**Post:** "One user said: 'I spent 4 hours/day on admin. Now 20 minutes.' Another: 'This is the first AI product that actually works for my specific business.'"

**Angle:** Social proof
**Objective:** Build trust
**CTA:** Try free

---

### Story 5: The Why (Day 5)
**Post:** "Why am I giving this away for free? Because I wish I had this 18 months ago. I'm validating demand before I price it. If it helps you, support me later."

**Angle:** Founder transparency
**Objective:** Remove skepticism
**CTA:** Try free, give feedback

---

### Story 6: The Invitation (Day 6)
**Post:** "I'm looking for 10 founding customers at $37 (regular $47). You get the full bundle + my direct support + input on next workflows. Help me make this better."

**Angle:** Co-creation
**Objective:** Convert to sale
**CTA:** DM "FOUNDING" to lock in

## Founder Voice Guidelines

- Be honest about struggles
- Use "I" not "we" (solopreneur)
- Show vulnerability
- Be specific with numbers
- Make it relatable
- End with offer
