# WARM NETWORK REACTIVATION PACKET

## Purpose
Reactivate warm contacts who already know you for high-ticket offers

---

## WARM NETWORK DEFINITION

- Past clients
- Colleagues
- Friends who run businesses
- LinkedIn connections
- Twitter mutuals
- Community members
- Anyone who's engaged with your content before

---

## REACTIVATION SCRIPTS

### Script 1: Past Client / Colleague
"Hey [name]! It's been a while. Hope you're good.

Quick update: I pivoted my business to help solopreneurs automate and systemize. Saved my last client £2,400/month.

Wanted to reach out because I think this could really help YOUR business. Do you have 10 minutes for a quick chat?"

---

### Script 2: Friend with Business
"Hey [name]! Been thinking of you.

I know you're [running your own business / consulting / etc]. I've been building some systems that could seriously help - freed up 12 hours/week for me.

Want to share what's working over coffee? My treat."

---

### Script 3: LinkedIn Connection
"Hey [name] - great connecting earlier. I help solopreneurs systemize and automate their businesses.

Curious - what's the biggest bottleneck in YOUR business right now? Could probably point you to some quick wins."

---

### Script 4: Engaged Follower (from posts)
"Hey [name] - noticed you've been following the AI workflow content. Thanks!

Quick question: what's the biggest time drain in your business? If it's anything like what I see, I might have a solution that could help."

---

## REACTIVATION SEQUENCE

### Day 0: First message
- Send to all warm contacts

### Day 1: Follow-up to non-responders
- "Hey - just following up on my last message"

### Day 3: Value addition
- Send helpful content (article, tip, resource)
- "Thought this might help with [their problem]"

### Day 5: Second attempt
- "Hey - one more thought. If you're still drowning in [problem], here's what usually works..."

## IF SOMEONE SAYS YES

**Step 1:** "Great! Let's do a quick 15-min call. [Suggest times]"

**Step 2:** Pre-qualify on call:
- "What's your biggest pain right now?"
- "How many hours are you working?"
- "What's your budget for solving this?"

**Step 3:** Pitch appropriate offer:
- L2: Product #1 if low budget
- L3: Bundle if medium interest
- L4: Premium service if high pain, budget

**Step 4:** Close: "Here's what I'd recommend... Want to proceed?"
