# REFERRAL INTRO PACKET

## Same-Day Referral Extraction

Turn every warm contact into a referral source.

---

## REFERRAL ASK SCRIPTS

### Script 1: The Direct Ask

```
[Name],

I'm looking for one more client today — anyone in your network who's looking to [systemize/scale/automate]?

If you know anyone, I'd genuinely appreciate an intro. I'll handle everything from there.

And if not, no worries at all.
```

---

### Script 2: The Value-First Ask

```
[Name],

Just helped [client] achieve [result] — wanted to share in case you know anyone who could benefit.

[One-sentence case study]

Anyone in your network who's dealing with [similar problem]?
```

---

### Script 3: The Contextual Ask

(When they're already talking about a problem)
```
"Knowing your network, who else do you know dealing with [same problem]?"
```

---

### Script 4: The Post-Conversation Ask

(After any positive conversation)
```
"Before you go — who else in your network might benefit from what we just discussed? I won't pressure them, just share what we do."
```

---

## REFERRAL RESPONSE HANDLING

**If they say "I know someone":**

```
That would be amazing. Can you make an intro?

I'll send them something light — no pressure at all, just opening a conversation.
```

**If they say "Let me think":**

```
Of course. When you think of someone, just drop me a message — I'll handle it from there.
```

**If they say "No, don't know anyone":**

```
No worries at all. If it comes to mind later, I'd genuinely appreciate it.
```

---

## REFERRAL FOLLOW-UP

**After they make intro:**

```
[Name],

Thanks for connecting us — I really appreciate it.

I'll reach out with a light touch — just sharing what I do.

[Your name]
```

**If referral responds:**

```
[Referral name] suggested we connect — said you might be dealing with [pain].

Worth a quick conversation?
```
