# Day 8-30 - Scale or Kill

## Decision Framework

### Scale Path (> 50 signals OR 3+ sales by Day 7)
1. Continue distribution at higher volume
2. Launch Product #2
3. Start email sequence
4. Build waitlist for future

### Pivot Path (25-50 signals, 0-1 sales)
1. Change offer angle
2. Test new CTA
3. Try new pricing
4. Keep testing

### Kill Path (< 25 signals)
1. Stop Product #1
2. Move to Product #2
3. Come back with new angle later

---

## Day 8-14: Optimization Phase

### Week 2 Focus
- Optimize CTAs based on data
- Test pricing variants
- Scale best channels
- Prepare Product #2 launch

### If Scaling
- 2x posting frequency
- New communities
- Run A/B tests on landing page

### If Struggling
- New offer angle
- Different price point
- Different audience

---

## Day 15-30: Expansion Phase

### If Validated (> 10 sales)
1. Launch Product #2
2. Create bundles
3. Start email nurturing
4. Consider paid promotion

### If Not Validated
1. Kill Product #1
2. Focus on Product #2
3. Try different niche

---

## Ongoing Tracking

- Weekly signal count
- Weekly sales count
- Revenue total
- Conversion rate trends
- Channel performance
