# FOUNDER OUTREACH £100K PACKET

## Target: Other Founders (Your Peer Group)

Founders understand founders. Use this for B2B founder-to-founder outreach.

---

## FOUNDER PROFILE

**Who to target:**
- Solo founders at £50K-£500K revenue
- Looking to scale to £1M+
- Have operational pain points
- Have budget (spends on tools/contractors)
- Active on LinkedIn/X

**Where to find them:**
- Your LinkedIn connections
- Founder communities
- Twitter followers
- Competitors' audiences
- Industry groups

---

## OUTREACH SEQUENCE

### Message 1: The Founder-to-Founder Hook

```
[Name],

Fellow founder here — saw your content [specific post/reason].

Quick question: what's your biggest operational pain right now? The thing that's keeping you from the next level?

Not selling — genuinely curious. Founders should help each other.

Reply with one word if you're open to sharing.
```

---

### Message 2: Value-First (If They Respond)

```
That's a common one. Here's what's worked for me...

[Specific tip based on their pain]

If you want to go deeper, I have a premium implementation that solves this — but I only work with founders who are ready to move.

Worth a conversation?
```

---

### Message 3: The Close

```
Based on our conversation, here's what I'd recommend...

[Custom offer based on their needs]

Investment: £15K-£30K depending on scope.

To move forward today, I'd need a deposit. What's your preference — transfer or PayPal?

Let me know and we'll get started.
```

---

## FOUNDERTALK LANGUAGE

### Use founder-to-founder framing

- "I've been where you are"
- "Here's what I did"
- "Founders should help each other"
- "No pitch — just value"
- "When you're ready"

### Avoid
- "Client" language initially
- "Buy this" language
- "Sales call" terminology
- "Limited time" (too salesy)

---

## CALL SCRIPTS FOR FOUNDERS

### Qualifying
"Tell me about where you are with [their pain point]"

### Value-Adding
"What if I showed you how [similar founder] solved this exact problem?"

### Presenting
"Based on what you've told me, here are your options..."

### Closing
"What would it take to get this done today?"

## CLOSE PLAYBOOK FOR FOUNDERS

**When they say "too expensive":**
"That's fair. Let's look at what happens if you don't solve this — what's the monthly cost of [their pain]?"

**When they say "not now":**
"What would need to happen for this to be a priority? Is there a specific event or deadline?"

**When they say "send a proposal":**
"Proposals take time to read. Let's do a 15-min call instead — I'll show you what's possible and you can decide."

**When they want to think:**
"What specifically do you need to think about? I can address it now."

## FOLLOW-UP PROTOCOL

- **Hour 2:** If no response, send value-add
- **Hour 4:** If no response, different angle
- **Hour 8:** Final check-in
- **Next day:** "Last message, then I'll stop"
