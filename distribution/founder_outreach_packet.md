# FOUNDER OUTREACH PACKET

## Purpose
Founder-to-founder direct outreach leveraging authentic founder story

---

## POSITIONING

You are a founder who found a solution. You're offering it to other founders who might benefit.

---

## OUTREACH CHANNELS

1. LinkedIn DMs
2. Twitter DMs
3. Email (if available)
4. Slack communities
5. Clubhouse/Spaces (later)

---

## FOUNDER STORY MESSAGE

### Variant A: The Burnout Story
"Hey [name], I burned out in 2024. 80-hour weeks, £3K/month on contractors, no time for my actual work.

So I built AI agents to do the work. Now I work 3 hours/day, saved £2,400/month.

I documented the whole system: 5 workflows that replace a VA for free.

If that sounds interesting, happy to share. This is what I wish I had 18 months ago."

### Variant B: The ROI Story
"Hey [name], quick context: I went from working 15-hour days to 3 hours by building AI workflows for my business.

The math: £2,400/month saved + 12 hours/week recovered = £28,800/year + 624 hours/year.

I'm now offering the system to other founders who are in the same spot. Want me to send it over?"

### Variant C: The Mutual Aid Story
"Hey [name], I know how tough it is running a one-person business. That's why I built a system to make it easier.

It's helped me and a few other founders save 12+ hours/week. Happy to share what worked for us.

If you're interested, just reply and I'll send you the playbook."

---

## SPECIFIC PLATFORMS

### LinkedIn Outreach
1. Find founders in your network (1st, 2nd degree)
2. Search by: founder, CEO, solopreneur, running a business
3. Personalize each message
4. Follow up in 48 hours

### Twitter/X Outreach
1. Find founders who tweet about working long hours
2. Reply to their posts with value
3. Then DM with offer
4. Or quote tweet with added value + DM

### Slack Communities
1. Join founder/solopreneur Slack groups
2. Provide value in channels
3. Then DM interested people
4. Don't spam - be genuinely helpful

---

## FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE

### If no response (Day 2-3)
"Hey - not trying to be pushy. Just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried. Let me know if you want the system or not - no pressure!"

### If they engaged but didn't buy (Day 5)
"Hey - checking in. Still interested in the AI workflow system? I can send you the details or hop on a quick call to answer questions."

### If they bought (Day 7+)
"Hey - glad you grabbed the bundle! Let me know how it's working. Also - if you want the full custom setup, I do that too (£1,500). Let me know!"

## WHAT TO OFFER

### L1: Free (if they seem interested but not ready)
"Here's the free workflow that started it all: [LINK]"

### L2: Low-ticket (if they want more)
"The full system is £37 - want the link?"

### L3: Bundle (if they see value)
"For £197 you get the full productivity stack - want details?"

### L4: Premium (if they have money pain)
"Actually, for a business your size, I'd recommend the custom setup (£1,500). Want to chat about it?"
