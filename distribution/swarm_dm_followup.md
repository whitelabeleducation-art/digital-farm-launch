# DM FOLLOW-UP LAUNCH SWARM

## Objective
Nurture all inbound leads through systematic follow-up sequences.

---

## Swarm Segments

### Segment A: Engaged But No DM
**Trigger:** Commented but didn't DM
**Follow-up:** 4 hours later

**Script:** "Hey - noticed you commented on the post. If you're curious about the AI agents, happy to answer questions! Just reply here."

---

### Segment B: DM But No Response
**Trigger:** Sent payment link, no response
**Follow-up:** 4 hours later, then 24 hours later

**Script:** "Hey - just checking if you got the link working? Any questions?"

---

### Segment C: Price Question
**Trigger:** Asked about price
**Follow-up:** After response, 24 hours later

**Script:** "Hey - still thinking it over? Founding price is $37 (that's $10 off). Let me know if you want in!"

---

### Segment D: Interested But Hesitant
**Trigger:** Said interested but didn't buy
**Follow-up:** 24 hours, 48 hours, 7 days

**Script:** "Hey - not trying to be pushy. Quick question: what's holding you back? I'm happy to help you figure out if this makes sense for you."

---

### Segment E: Downloaded But No Engagement
**Trigger:** Downloaded free but no follow-up
**Follow-up:** 48 hours later

**Script:** "Hey - got the bundle working? Which workflow are you most excited about? Let me know if you have questions!"

---

### Segment F: Post-Sale
**Trigger:** Completed purchase
**Follow-up:** 1 hour, 3 days, 7 days, 14 days

**Scripts:**
- 1 hour: "Got it! Let me know if you have questions!"
- 3 days: "How's it going? Try any of the workflows yet?"
- 7 days: "Would love your feedback - what works, what could be better?"
- 14 days: "If this helped, a quick testimonial would be amazing!"
