# Day 1-7 - Build Momentum

## Daily Routine

### Each Morning
1. Check signal log
2. Review Day -1 engagement
3. Post new content (repurposed)
4. Send follow-ups to Day -1 engagers

### Each Afternoon
1. Process all DMs
2. Send payment links
3. Close any warm leads

### Each Evening
1. Log day's signals
2. Plan tomorrow's angles
3. Update conversion notes

---

## Day 1: Double Down on What Worked

- Review Day 0 data
- Identify top-performing post
- Repost to new communities with same angle

---

## Day 2: New Angle Test

- Try different hook (pain vs ROI vs curiosity)
- New communities (r/entrepreneur, r/contentmarketing)

---

## Day 3: Cross-Sell Tease

- Mention Product #2 in posts
- "One more thing for content creators..."

---

## Day 4: Proof Point

- Share early results/testimonials
- "One person said..."

---

## Day 5: Founder Story

- Full founder story post
- Build trust, humanize

---

## Day 6: Urgency Push

- "Founding round ends tonight"
- Direct ask to convert

---

## Day 7: Decision Point

- Evaluate: Scale OR Pivot OR Kill
- If > 50 signals: Scale
- If 25-50: Keep testing
- If < 25: Kill or new angle
