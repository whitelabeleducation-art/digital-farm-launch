# CTA TESTS - Call to Action Optimization

## Current CTA
"Get the workflows" / "Download now"

## Test Variants

### Variant A: Outcome
- "Reclaim 12 hours/week"
- "Cut your workload in half"
- "Save $2,400/month"

### Variant B: Action
- "Get the system"
- "Start now - free"
- "Try it now"

### Variant C: Urgency
- "Last 10 spots at $37"
- "Free for 24 hours"
- "Founding round closing"

### Variant D: Transformation
- "Work 3 hours, not 15"
- "Escape the busy trap"
- "Become a 4-hour entrepreneur"

### Variant E: Direct
- "Buy now - $37"
- "Get instant access"
- "Pay $37, save 12 hours"

## Best Performers (Predicted)

**For cold traffic:** "Get the system - free"
**For warm traffic:** "Last 10 spots at $37"
**For hot traffic:** "Buy now - $37"

## Implementation

Use in order:
1. Reddit: Variant B (direct, low friction)
