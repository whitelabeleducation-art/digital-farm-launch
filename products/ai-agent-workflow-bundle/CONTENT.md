## Module 1: Foundation

### Chapter 1: Understanding Automation for Solopreneurs

As a solopreneur, your time is your most valuable asset. Every hour spent on repetitive tasks is an hour not spent on revenue-generating activities.

This module helps you identify which tasks deserve automation and which require the human touch.

### Key Principles:
- Rule of 3: If you do it 3+ times, automate it
- Start with high-frequency tasks
- Build for scale, not just efficiency

### Action Items:
1. List your top 10 weekly tasks
2. Mark each as: Manual / Can Automate / Should Automate
3. Rank by time spent and frequency
