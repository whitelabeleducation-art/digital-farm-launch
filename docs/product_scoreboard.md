## Product Scoring Framework

### Scoring Criteria (1-10 scale)

**Build Speed** (How fast can we create this?)
- 10: Copy-paste templates, hours to create
- 5: Moderate content creation, days
- 1: Complex development, weeks

**Validation Speed** (How fast can we validate demand?)
- 10: Immediate - comments, upvotes, DM interest
- 5: Requires list building or content
- 1: Requires product launch

**Willingness to Pay** (Evidence of paying customers?)
- 10: Direct purchases or clear purchase intent
- 5: Pain acknowledged but no purchase evidence
- 1: Free solutions preferred

**Competition Saturation** (How crowded is the market?)
- 10: Saturated - hard to differentiate
- 5: Some competition but room exists
- 1: Open market

**Differentiation Potential** (Can we stand out?)
- 10: Unique angle clearly possible
- 5: Some differentiation possible
- 1: Hard to differentiate

**Distribution Ease** (How easy to reach audience?)
- 10: Easy - active communities, clear channels
- 5: Requires content creation
- 1: Hard to reach audience

**Expansion Potential** (Can we build on this?)
- 10: Clear ladder, bundle opportunities
- 5: Some expansion possible
- 1: Limited growth

### Product Selection Formula
Final Score = (Build + Validation + Willingness + Differentiation + Distribution + Expansion) / 6
Saturation Penalty = -1 if saturated > 7

## Kill Criteria
- Score < 5 after validation attempt
- No demand signal after 2 weeks of distribution
- Unable to differentiate from competition

## Scale Criteria
- Score > 7.5
- Clear demand signals
- Clear expansion opportunity
