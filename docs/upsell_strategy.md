# UPSELL STRATEGY

## When to Upsell

### Trigger 1: Post-Purchase (Day 1-3)
Customer just bought Product #1.

**Upsell:** Product #2 (cross-sell) at $17 (founding) or Product #3 (premium) at $47.

**Script:**
"Hey! Since you grabbed the [Product #1], I also built [Product #2/3]. It's [description]. For founding customers, it's [price] instead of [regular]. Want to add it?"

---

### Trigger 2: Positive Feedback (Day 3-7)
Customer loves Product #1, gives good feedback.

**Upsell:** Product #3 (premium).

**Script:**
"Glad it's working! One more thing - I also built [Product #3] for solopreneurs who want to scale to $10K/month. It's [price] for founding customers. Want details?"

---

### Trigger 3: Expressed Need (Any time)
Customer mentions wanting more: "I wish there was something for X"

**Upsell:** Related product or premium tier.

**Script:**
"Great idea! I actually have [Product #3] which covers exactly that. It's [price] and includes [features]. Want me to send you the details?"

---

### Trigger 4: Bundle Opportunity (Day 14+)
Customer has bought multiple, hasn't seen bundle.

**Upsell:** Bundle deal.

**Script:**
"I noticed you have [Products]. For [total price], you can get the full Productivity Stack and save [amount]. It's everything you need. Want in?"

---

## Upsell Script Library

### Script A: Cross-sell (Quick)
"By the way - I also built [Product #2]. It's $17 for founding customers (regular $27). Want me to add it?"

### Script B: Premium Upsell
"If you're serious about [goal], check out [Product #3]. It shows exactly how to [result]. It's $47 (or $97 with my 1-on-1 help)."

### Script C: Bundle
"Here's a deal: get [Product #1] + [Product #2] for $57 instead of $74. Save $17. Want me to send the link?"

### Script D: Time-Sensitive
"Quick update - founding price on [Product #3] goes up in 48 hours. $47 while it lasts. Want in?"

---

## Upsell Response Options

| Response | Action |
|----------|--------|
| "Yes" | Send payment link |
| "Maybe later" | Note in CRM, follow up in 7 days |
| "No thanks" | Note, don't push, still provide great support |
| "Too much" | Offer smaller item or payment plan |

## Upsell Performance Goals

- **Cross-sell rate:** 30% of Product #1 buyers
- **Premium upsell rate:** 15% of engaged customers
- **Bundle conversion:** 20% of customers offered
