# DOWNSELL STRATEGY

## When to Downsell

### Scenario 1: Price Objection
Customer says "too expensive" or "can't afford"

**Downsell Options:**
- Offer lower tier (if exists)
- Offer payment plan
- Offer free version
- Offer one workflow instead of all 5

**Script:**
"I hear you. Here's what I can do: 
- Try the free version first (link in post)
- Or get just ONE workflow for $17 instead of $37
- Or pay $27 now, $27 in 30 days

What's most comfortable for you?"

---

### Scenario 2: Overwhelmed Customer
Customer seems unsure, needs simpler entry

**Downsell:** Entry product only, no premium features

**Script:**
"Let's start small. Try Product #1 first - it's $37 and covers the basics. Once you see it working, you can upgrade to the premium. Sound good?"

---

### Scenario 3: Testing the Waters
Customer wants to try before committing

**Downsell:** Free version, trial, or lower price

**Script:**
"100% - try it first and see if it works for you. The free version is in the post. If it helps and you want the full setup guide later, I'm here."

---

### Scenario 4: Wrong Fit
Customer's needs don't match current product

**Downsell:** Direct to better-fit product or recommend competitor

**Script:**
"Just to check - are you looking for [X] or [Y]? Because [Product #3] might be a better fit for what you're describing. Want me to tell you about that instead?"

---

## Downsell Pricing Tiers

| Level | Price | What's Included |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | Basic info, sample workflow |
| Lite | $17 | One workflow + basic guide |
| Standard | $27-37 | Full product, no premium |
| Premium | $47-97 | Full + extras + support |

---

## Downsell Response Handling

| Customer Says | Our Response |
|---------------|--------------|
| "Too expensive" | "Try free first, or lite version at $17" |
| "Need to think" | "Bookmark this - let me know when ready" |
| "Not right for me" | "What ARE you looking for? I might have it" |
| "Can you do better?" | "Founding price IS the deal - $37 is already discounted" |
| "Just looking" | "No pressure! Let me know if you have questions" |
