# Scale Playbook

## When to Scale

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| > 50 signals in 7 days | Scale distribution |
| > 5 sales in 7 days | Full launch mode |
| > 20 sales in 14 days | Product #2 launch |
| > 50 sales in 30 days | Bundle promotion |
| Negative trends | Kill/switch angle |

---

## How to Scale Distribution

### Scale Up
- Post to more communities
- Post more frequently
- Test new angles
- Run paid promotion (later)

### Scale Out
- Launch Product #2
- Cross-sell to existing customers
- Build email list
- Build waitlist

### Scale Deep
- Optimize conversions
- Improve offer
- Test pricing
- Build bundles

---

## Kill Criteria

| Signal | Time | Action |
|--------|------|--------|
| < 10 signals | 7 days | Kill product |
| < 25 signals | 14 days | Switch angle |
| No sales | 14 days | Kill |
| Negative conversion | 7 days | Change offer |
