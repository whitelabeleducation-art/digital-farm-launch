# Content Engine - Production System

## Content Types

### 1. Launch Posts
- Purpose: Drive initial awareness
- Format: Problem → Solution → CTA
- Channels: Reddit, X, LinkedIn
- Frequency: Day 0 of launch, repurpose Week 1

### 2. Educational Posts
- Purpose: Build authority, SEO
- Format: "How to [X] in [Y] steps"
- Channels: Blog, X, Reddit
- Frequency: 2-3x/week

### 3. Authority Posts
- Purpose: Prove expertise
- Format: "The truth about [X]" / "[X] is dead, here's what's next"
- Channels: X, LinkedIn
- Frequency: 1-2x/week

### 4. Founder Story Posts
- Purpose: Humanize, build trust
- Format: "I used to [pain], now I [result]"
- Channels: X, LinkedIn
- Frequency: 1-2x/week

### 5. Objection-Handling Posts
- Purpose: Pre-handle concerns
- Format: "But what about [objection]?"
- Channels: X, Comments
- Frequency: As needed

### 6. Customer Problem Posts
- Purpose: Connect with audience
- Format: Describe their exact pain
- Channels: Reddit, X
- Frequency: 3-4x/week

### 7. Lead Magnet Content
- Purpose: Capture emails
- Format: Mini-guide, checklist, template
- Channels: Landing page, X bio, Reddit
- Frequency: Ongoing

### 8. Short-Form Content
- Purpose: High engagement, reach
- Format: 1-2 sentences, hooks
- Channels: X, TikTok
- Frequency: Daily

### 9. Long-Form Content
- Purpose: Deep authority, SEO
- Format: 1000-2000 word guides
- Channels: Blog, Medium
- Frequency: 1-2x/month

### 10. Marketplace Content
- Purpose: Product listing visibility
- Format: Gumroad/Ko-fi product descriptions
- Channels: Gumroad, Ko-fi
- Frequency: Per product

---

## Content Production Pipeline

### Phase 1: Launch Content (Per Product)
1. Launch post (Day 0)
2. Detail post (Day 2)
3. Proof post (Day 4)
4. Audience post (Day 7)
5. Authority post (Day 14)

### Phase 2: Ongoing Content (Weekly)
1. Educational post (Mon)
2. Founder story (Wed)
3. Problem post (Fri)
4. Short-form daily

### Phase 3: Evergreen Content (Monthly)
1. Guide (Month start)
2. Case study (Month mid)
3. Checklist (Month end)
