# OFFER LADDER - Product #1 to #3

## Product Positioning

### Product #1: Entry Level
- **Name:** The 3-Hour Workday System
- **Price:** $37 (founding) / $47 (regular)
- **Promise:** Time freedom
- **Audience:** Solopreneurs drowning in admin
- **Role:** Lead capture, customer acquisition

### Product #2: Cross-Sell (Natural Pair)
- **Name:** Notion Content Creator OS
- **Price:** $27 (founding) / $47 (regular)
- **Promise:** Content organization
- **Audience:** Content creators (overlaps with #1)
- **Role:** Cross-sell, bundle component

### Product #3: Upsell (Premium)
- **Name:** Solopreneur Profit System
- **Price:** $47 (founding) / $97 (regular)
- **Promise:** Revenue growth ($10K/month)
- **Audience:** Growth-focused solopreneurs
- **Role:** Premium upgrade, upsell

---

## Ladder Logic

### Entry → Cross-sell
**When:** Customer buys Product #1
**Offer:** "Since you're saving time, you might want to stay organized too. The Notion Content Creator OS is $27 (or $47 if you want the premium). Want me to add it?"

**Bundle deal:** "For $57 you can get BOTH - that's $17 off buying separately."

---

### Entry → Premium Upsell
**When:** Customer buys Product #1, shows growth interest
**Offer:** "If you're serious about scaling, the Solopreneur Profit System shows you how to get to $10K/month. It's $47 (or $97 with a 1-on-1 call). Want details?"

**Logic:** Product #3 is for customers who want MORE than time savings - they want MONEY.

---

### Cross-sell → Premium
**When:** Customer buys Product #2
**Offer:** "Now that your content is organized, the next step is turning that content into revenue. The Profit System shows you how. $47 or $97 with call."

---

## Price Gap Rules

- **Small gap** (Entry → Cross-sell): $10 difference = easy add-on
- **Large gap** (Entry → Premium): $60 difference = needs justification
- **Bundle** (All three): $97 for all three = best value

## Offer Sequence by Day

| Day | Customer State | Offer |
|-----|----------------|-------|
| 0 | Just downloaded #1 | Cross-sell #2 if relevant |
| 3 | Used #1 | Ask about #2 if not bought |
| 7 | Feedback received | Upsell #3 if positive |
| 14 | Active user | Bundle offer if not already |

## Rejection Handling

- "Too much" → "Try #2 first for $27"
- "Not ready" → "Let me know when you are"
- "Not relevant" → "No problem - let me know if anything changes"
