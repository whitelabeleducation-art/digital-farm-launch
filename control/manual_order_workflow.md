# MANUAL ORDER WORKFLOW

## Exact Process

### 1. ORDER RECEIVED
**Trigger:** Customer says "I want to buy" / "send me the link" / "how do I pay"

**Action:** Immediately respond (within 2 minutes)

---

### 2. SEND PAYMENT LINK
**Option A (if checkout link exists):**
"Here's your checkout link: [LINK]. Pay $37 and you'll get the bundle instantly."

**Option B (if PayPal.Me exists):**
"Here's your PayPal link: paypal.me/me/37 - send $37 and I'll send the bundle."

**Option C (manual):**
"I'll send you a PayPal request now - check your email!"

---

### 3. CONFIRM PAYMENT
**Wait:** Up to 10 minutes
**Check:** Payment notification

**If no payment after 10 min:**
"Hey - just checking if you got the payment link working?"

---

### 4. PAYMENT CONFIRMED
**Action:** Send bundle immediately

**Bundle location:** products/ai-agent-workflow-bundle/CONTENT.md

**Script:**
"Got it! Payment confirmed. Here's your bundle: [DOWNLOAD LINK]

QUICK START:
1. Try the Research Agent first
2. Copy the prompt into ChatGPT/Claude
3. Test it with your own work

Let me know if you have questions!"

---

### 5. POST-SALE NURTURE
**Day 1:** "Hey - just checking you got the bundle! Let me know if you have questions."

**Day 3:** "How's it going? Try any of the workflows yet?"

**Day 7:** "Would love your feedback - what works, what could be better?"

**Day 14:** "If this helped you, a quick testimonial would be amazing!"

---
