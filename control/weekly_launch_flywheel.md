# Weekly Launch Flywheel

## The Loop (Per Product)

### Week 1: Launch & Capture
- Day 0: Post to all channels (Reddit, X, LinkedIn)
- Day 1: Monitor DMs, reply fast, log signals
- Day 2: Repost to different communities
- Day 3: Send follow-up to Day 0 engagers
- Day 4: Post new angle/variant
- Day 5: Test different CTA
- Day 6: Retarget engaged users
- Day 7: Scale what works, kill what doesn't

### Week 2: Optimize & Expand
- Day 8: Update copy based on Week 1 learnings
- Day 9: Launch to new communities
- Day 10: Test offer variant
- Day 11: Cross-sell to engaged users
- Day 12: Launch email sequence
- Day 13: Test price variant
- Day 14: Decision: Scale OR pivot OR kill

### Week 3+: Compound or Kill
- If scaling: Double down on top channels
- If pivoting: New angle, new communities
- If killing: Move to next product

## Product Rotation

| Week | Focus Product | Secondary Support |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| 1 | #1 | #2 prep |
| 2 | #1 (if validates) | #2 launch |
| 3 | #2 | #3 prep |
| 4 | #2 or #3 | #1 bundles |

---

## Flywheel Mechanics

1. **Launch post** → Engagement
2. **Engagement** → Email/Lead capture
3. **Lead** → Nurture sequence
4. **Nurture** → Cross-sell or upsell
5. **Cross-sell** → New product launch
6. **New launch** → More engagement
7. **More engagement** → More leads → More sales

Each product feeds the next.
