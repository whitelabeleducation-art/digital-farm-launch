# Daily Distribution Engine

## Daily Workflow

### Morning Block (9am-11am)
1. Post to primary channel (based on weekly rotation)
2. Monitor replies, comments
3. Reply to overnight engagement

### Midday Block (12pm-2pm)
4. Process all DMs
5. Update validation scorecard
6. Log new signals

### Afternoon Block (3pm-5pm)
7. Post to secondary channel
8. Send follow-ups to Day -1 engagers
9. Update landing page CTAs if needed

### Evening Block (6pm-8pm)
10. Final post of day (X/LinkedIn)
11. Check final signals
12. Plan tomorrow's angles

## Content Types by Channel

### Reddit
- Problem-led posts
- "How I fixed X" stories
- Questions to the community
- Templates/resources sharing

### X/Twitter
- Hook tweets
- Thread deep-dives
- Thread: "How I did X"
- Quick tips
- Engagement bait

### LinkedIn
- Business-focused narratives
- ROI/result stories
- Professional angles

---

## Repost Rules

- Same content: 48-hour minimum gap
- Same channel: 72-hour minimum gap
- New angle: Same day allowed
- Evergreen: Repost weekly

---

## Batch Posting Mode

When multiple products launching:
- Morning: Product #1
- Afternoon: Product #2
- Evening: Both cross-sells

---

## Signal Tracking

Every post track:
- Impressions (if available)
- Replies
- DMs
- Link clicks
- Conversions
