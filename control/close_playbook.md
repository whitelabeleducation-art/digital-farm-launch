# Close Playbook

## High Intent Close

### When they say "I want to buy" or "send me the link"
**Step 1:** "Awesome! Here's your payment link: [LINK]"

**Step 2:** "Founding price is $37 (regular $47). Use code FOUNDING20 at checkout."

**Step 3:** "Once you pay, I'll send the full bundle to your email immediately."

**Step 4:** [After payment] "Got it! Here's your bundle: [DOWNLOAD LINK]. Quick start: try the Research Agent first. Let me know how it goes!"

---

## Warm Close

### When they've engaged multiple times but haven't bought
**Step 1:** "I've noticed you've been following along - thanks for that!"

**Step 2:** "Founder pricing is still available - $37 for the full bundle. Want me to send you the link?"

**Step 3:** "No pressure at all. But if you're curious, now's the time."

---

## Scarcity Close

### When they're on the fence
**Step 1:** "Quick update - we're down to 8 founding spots at $37."

**Step 2:** "After those are gone, it's $47 and regular pricing."

**Step 3:** "Want to lock one in?"

---

## Risk Reversal Close

### When they're hesitant
**Step 1:** "Here's what I'll do: Try the free version first. If it doesn't save you 2+ hours in the first week, I'll pay you $10."

**Step 2:** "That's how confident I am. You have nothing to lose."

**Step 3:** "Sound fair?"

---

## Cross-Sell Close

### When they just bought Product #1
**Step 1:** "Glad you grabbed the bundle! One more thing..."

**Step 2:** "I also built the Notion Content Creator OS - keeps all your content organized. Same founder pricing."

**Step 3:** "For $57 you can get both - that's $25 off. Want to add it?"

---

## Bundle Close

### When they want more value
**Step 1:** "For $64, you get the AI Agent Bundle AND the Notion Content Creator OS."

**Step 2:** "That's everything you need to save time AND stay organized."

**Step 3:** "Compare to buying separately: $74. You're saving $10."

---

## Follow-Up After Close

### Same day
"Thanks again! Let me know how the workflows work for you. Happy to help if you get stuck."

### 3 days later
"Hey - just checking - did you get a chance to try the workflows? Any questions?"

### 7 days later
"How's it going? Would love to hear what's working and what could be better. Your feedback makes the product better!"

### 14 days later
"If this helped you, I'd really appreciate a quick testimonial. It helps others decide!"
