## WHEN TO USE EACH

### High-Ticket (Tier 1)
**Trigger:** Business owner, 40+ hours worked, willing to invest, clear pain
**Channel:** Direct message, call
**Objection handling:** Price math, ROI, risk reversal

### Mid-Ticket (Tier 2)
**Trigger:** Already engaged, showed interest, medium budget
**Channel:** DM, email
**Objection handling:** Value vs price, bundle savings

### Low-Ticket (Tier 3)
**Trigger:** Any interest, any budget
**Channel:** Any
**Objection handling:** Try free, founding discount
