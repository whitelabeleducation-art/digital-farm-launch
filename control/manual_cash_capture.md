## OBJECTION HANDLING

### "Too expensive"
**Response:** "Compared to what? What does [their pain] cost per month? What's the ROI if solved?"

### "Need to think"
**Response:** "What specifically do you need to think about? I can address it now."

### "Not ready"
**Response:** "What would 'ready' look like? What's the gap?"

### "Send proposal"
**Response:** "Proposals are one-way. A 15-min call is two-way — I can answer your questions. Can you do a call today?"

### "Need to consult partner"
**Response:** "What would help them say yes? I can help make the case."

### "Can you do it for less?"
**Response:** "Let's look at scope — we could [reduce deliverables], that brings it to £[lower]. Or we start with £2,500 audit and upgrade later."

### "Not a priority right now"
**Response:** "What would make it a priority? Is there a specific event or deadline?"

## DEPOSIT WORKFLOW

### When They Say Yes:

1. **Confirm:** "Great — let me get you the details"
2. **Present:** "Deposit of £[X] to reserve your spot"
3. **Method:** "Bank transfer or PayPal?"
4. **Send:** Payment details / link
5. **Confirm:** "Got it — you're in. Here's what's next..."

### When They Hesitate:

1. **Acknowledge:** "I understand — this is a big decision"
2. **Probe:** "What's holding you back?"
3. **Address:** Handle objection
4. **Retry:** "So should we move forward?"

### When They Say No:

1. **Accept:** "No problem — I appreciate your time"
2. **Follow-up:** "If anything changes, I'm here"
3. **Referral:** "Know anyone else who might benefit?"
