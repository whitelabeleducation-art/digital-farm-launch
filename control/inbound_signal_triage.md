# VALIDATION SIGNAL TRIAGE

## Signal Classification

### TIER 1: WEAK SIGNALS (1-2 points)
| Signal | Score | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| View/like | 1 | Minimal interest |
| Bookmark | 1 | Future consideration |
| Generic comment | 1 | Casual engagement |
| "Interesting" | 1 | Not committed |

**Action:** Note, don't pursue aggressively

---

### TIER 2: WARM SIGNALS (3-5 points)
| Signal | Score | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Question in comments | 3 | Considering |
| DM with question | 3 | Active interest |
| Comment "how do I get this" | 3 | Wants info |
| Email captured | 2 | Willing to give contact |
| Reply to CTA | 3 | Action taken |

**Action:** Send information, build relationship

---

### TIER 3: STRONG SIGNALS (6-9 points)
| Signal | Score | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| DM "interested" | 6 | Real interest |
| Asked about price | 6 | Evaluating |
| DM "tell me more" | 5 | Deepening interest |
| Multiple engagements | 5 | High consideration |
| Shared post | 4 | Endorsed |

**Action:** Send payment link, offer founding price

---

### TIER 4: SALES-READY SIGNALS (10+ points)
| Signal | Score | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| DM "I want this" | 10 | Ready to buy |
| DM "send me the link" | 10 | Ready to buy |
| DM "how do I pay" | 10 | Ready to buy |
| Preorder requested | 7 | Committed intent |
| Asked about buying | 8 | High intent |

**Action:** Send payment link immediately, close

---

### TIER 5: HIGH-VALUE LEADS (Bonus)
| Signal | Score | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Has large following | +3 | Amplifier |
| Is a potential partner | +5 | Distribution |
| Runs a community | +4 | Platform |
| Has budget for more | +3 | Upsell potential |

**Action:** Nurture specially, potential for bundle/upsell

---

## Signal-to-Action Matrix

| Tier | Signal Type | Response Time | Primary Action |
|------|-------------|---------------|----------------|
| 1 | View/like | None | Log only |
| 1 | Bookmark | None | Log only |
| 2 | Question | < 1 hour | Answer, offer info |
| 2 | Email captured | < 2 hours | Send bundle + welcome |
| 3 | DM interested | < 30 min | Send payment link |
| 3 | Asked price | < 15 min | Send payment link + offer |
| 4 | Want to buy | < 5 min | Send payment link immediately |
| 4 | Preorder | < 10 min | Confirm + add to list |
| 5 | High-value | < 1 hour | Personal outreach |

## Inbound Triage Protocol

### Step 1: Classify Signal
- Read message
- Assign tier
- Assign score

### Step 2: Determine Response
- Select script from dm_closers.md or objection_library.md

### Step 3: Execute Response
- Send within response time SLA

### Step 4: Log Signal
- Update validation_signals.md
- Update validation_scorecard.md

### Step 5: Follow Up
- Schedule follow-up based on tier
